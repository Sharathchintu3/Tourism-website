PLACES IN GOA

1.CALANGUTTE BEACH

Calangute Beach is one of the most popular and bustling beaches in North Goa, India. Known for its vibrant atmosphere, water sports, and a plethora of beach shacks, Calangute attracts both domestic and international tourists.

2.FORT AGUADA

Fort Aguada was built by the Portuguese in the early 17th century, primarily to defend against Dutch and Marathas. The fort served as a crucial defense outpost for the Portuguese, and its name "Aguada" is derived from the freshwater spring within the fort.

Taj Exotica is situated along the pristine Benaulim Beach, offering guests a tranquil and picturesque setting away from the more bustling areas of North Goa.The resort boasts well-appointed rooms, suites, and villas designed for comfort and luxury.

5.BOM JESUS BASILICA

The construction of the Bom Jesus Basilica was completed in 1605. It holds immense historical significance as it houses the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier, a prominent Jesuit missionary who played a crucial role in spreading Christianity in Asia during the 16th century.
