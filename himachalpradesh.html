1.MANALI

Manali is a popular hill station and tourist destination located in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India. Nestled in the Pir Panjal and Dhauladhar ranges of the Himalayas, Manali is known for its breathtaking landscapes, lush green valleys, and snow-capped mountains

Kasol is a small village nestled in the Parvati Valley of the Kullu district in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India. Known for its stunning landscapes, serene ambiance, and vibrant culture, Kasol has gained popularity as a backpacker's paradise and a haven for nature lovers.

4.SPITI VALLEY

Spiti Valley, located in the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, is a remote and breathtakingly beautiful mountain desert. Tucked away in the Trans-Himalayan region, Spiti is known for its stark landscapes, high mountain passes, ancient monasteries, and a unique cultural blend.

Dalhousie is a charming hill station nestled in the Dhauladhar mountain range in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Known for its scenic landscapes, colonial architecture, and pleasant weather, Dalhousie is a popular tourist destination.
