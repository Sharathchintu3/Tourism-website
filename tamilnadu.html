PLACES IN TAMILNADU

1.KODAIKANAL

Kodaikanal is a charming hill station located in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Situated in the Palani Hills of the Western Ghats, Kodaikanal is known for its pleasant climate, scenic landscapes, and a variety of tourist attractions.

2.OOTY

Nestled amidst Nilgiri hills, Ooty, also known as Udagamandalam, is a hill station in Tamil Nadu which serves as a top-rated tourist destination. Once regarded as the summer headquarters of the East India Company, the Queen of the hills is a picturesque getaway.

3.RAMESWARAM

Munnar is a picturesque hill station located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, South India. Known for its lush tea plantations, misty hills, and serene landscapes, Munnar is a popular destination for nature lovers, honeymooners, and those seeking a tranquil escape.

4.PONDICHERRY

Pondicherry, officially known as Puducherry, is a Union Territory in India located on the southeastern coast, bordered by the state of Tamil Nadu. Steeped in history and known for its unique blend of French and Indian cultures, Pondicherry attracts visitors with its colonial architecture, spiritual atmosphere, and coastal charm.

5.KANYAKUMARI

Kanyakumari, located at the southernmost tip of the Indian subcontinent, is a town in the state of Tamil Nadu. Known for its stunning natural beauty, cultural significance, and the confluence of three major water bodies—the Bay of Bengal, the Arabian Sea, and the Indian Ocean—Kanyakumari is a popular tourist destination.
